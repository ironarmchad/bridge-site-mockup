Welcome to Bridge Christian Church. We’re glad you are here! We are a diverse group of people made up of families and individuals of all ages, from many different church backgrounds who are trying to live in Christian community. Whether you’ve attended church all your life or you’ve never been in church, we invite you to experience the difference here.



Parking


At Bridge you will find special parking reserved for first time guests. Simply turn on your hazard lights as you turn into the parking lot, so that the parking attendants will recognize you as a first time guest. Follow the signs to designated parking.



Worship Services


  • 4 Identical Services - 8:15, 9:45 & 11:15am on Sunday's and 6:30pm on Thursday's
  • Contemporary, energetic worship led by our Creative Arts Pastor and praise band
  • Messages relevant to your daily life
  • Weekly Communion—following the sermon
  • Casual and comfortable attire​
  • Hosts at the Guest Services area to assist you with questions and show you around our facility
  • Complimentary coffee available in the foyer
  • Nursery and children's classes (age 2-5th grade) taught by screened and trained volunteers during each service
  • Middle School class offered during the 9:45 service
  • A special gift for you in Meeting Room A
