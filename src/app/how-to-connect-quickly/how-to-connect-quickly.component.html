Starting Point

Starting Point is a relaxed two-week study that will open the doors to our mission, strategy, beliefs and goals for your future here at Bridge Christian Church. The class concludes with an introduction to Connect Groups, Life Groups, ministry opportunities and lunch with the ministry staff. Starting Point meets every other month in meeting room A during the 11:15am worship service and is led by a Bridge staff member. To sign-up for the next Starting Point Class, register below.
